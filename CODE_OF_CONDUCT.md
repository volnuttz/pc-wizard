# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in pc-wizard welcoming and respectful
for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or ability.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and inclusive.
- Give and receive feedback in good faith.
- Focus criticism on ideas, behavior, and code rather than people.
- Respect privacy and do not share another person's private information.
- Follow the project's scope and source-of-truth guidance.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, sexualized conduct,
unwelcome contact, doxxing, deliberate disruption, and other conduct that would
make a reasonable participant feel unsafe are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report concerns privately to the repository maintainers through the contact
method listed in the repository's GitHub profile. Do not open a public issue for
sensitive reports. Maintainers may remove comments, restrict participation, or
take other action appropriate to the situation.

Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical. Retaliation against a
person who raises a concern in good faith is not acceptable.

This code of conduct is adapted for this project from the Contributor Covenant
principles and applies to project spaces and project-related interactions.
